This is a web-based homage to the Dreamachine invented by Brion Gysin and Ian Sommerville.

The Dreamachine is a stroboscopic flickering light art device that produces eidetic visual stimuli.

The idea is that the pulsations of light correspond to the electrical oscillations normally present in the human brain while relaxing, inducing an alpha wave, hypanagogic mental state.

As with the Dreamachine, the light fluctuations of this web page are intended to be viewed with the eyes closed.
